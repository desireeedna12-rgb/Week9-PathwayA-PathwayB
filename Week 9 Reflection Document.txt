I would update my documentation as I completed each requirement instead of waiting until everything was finished. I would also test my application after every small change so I could find problems sooner instead of troubleshooting multiple changes at once. I would also make sure that i confirmed everything is in GitHub, making sure that my readme.txt states how my professor can find my assignments. Finally, I would ask questions earlier whenever I was unsure about the assignment requirements or where new code belonged, because getting help sooner would have saved time and made the development process smoother.
